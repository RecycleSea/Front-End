HOW IT WORKS?
Collect ocean waste
Fishermen, coastal communities, and volunteers collect plastic waste from the ocean.
Upload & Get Valuation
Users upload photos, weight, and location of collected waste. The app estimates its value.
Sell & Earn Rewards
Once verified, users receive payments via e-wallet, or redeemable eco-points.
Recyclers Purchase Materials
Industries browse and buy sorted ocean plastic for recycling.
